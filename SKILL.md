## What This Skill Does

Generates a complete local marketing content package for healthcare and medical practices — content that is patient-friendly, HIPAA-safe, and designed to attract new patients and increase appointment bookings.

**Four production-ready outputs:**
1. **New Patient Welcome Kit** — welcome letter, intake FAQ, and 5-message onboarding sequence
2. **Service Page Copy** — optimized procedure descriptions for your top 3 services with search intent targeting
3. **Review & Reputation System** — 20 HIPAA-safe review request templates + 10 response templates
4. **30-Day Social Calendar** — content calendar with health awareness posts, staff spotlights, and patient education (no PHI)

---

## The 4 Prompts

### Prompt 1 — New Patient Welcome Kit (FREE)
**Best for:** New patient retention, reducing no-shows, building trust from day one

**Input:**
- Practice name & specialty (dental, chiro, urgent care, med spa, etc.)
- City/neighborhood
- Key differentiators (latest technology, same-day appointments, accepts X insurance, etc.)
- Tone (warm/clinical/premium)

**Output:**
- Welcome letter (2 paragraphs, patient-friendly, no medical claims)
- Intake FAQ (10 Q&As: what to bring, what to expect, payment/insurance, cancellation)
- 5-message welcome email sequence (Day 0 confirmation → Day-1 reminder → Day of → Day 7 follow-up → Day 30 re-engagement)
- Appointment confirmation SMS (under 160 chars, TCPA-compliant)

---

### Prompt 2 — Service Page Copy
**Best for:** Website SEO, Google Ads landing pages, procedure awareness

**Input:**
- Practice specialty and top 3 services/procedures to describe
- Ideal patient (age, condition, concern)
- Any unique technology or approach
- Desired tone

**Output (per service):**
- SEO title + meta description (60 + 160 chars)
- H1 headline + above-the-fold paragraph (patient benefit-focused, no unsubstantiated health claims)
- "What to Expect" section (numbered steps, anxiety-reducing)
- "Who Is This For?" qualifying section
- FAQ block (5 Q&As, schema markup included)
- CTA with appointment trigger phrase

---

### Prompt 3 — Review & Reputation System
**Best for:** Google Business Profile star rating, Yelp reputation, new patient trust signals

**Input:**
- Practice name and specialty
- Top 3 outcomes/compliments patients give
- Review platforms to target (Google, Healthgrades, Zocdoc, Yelp)
- Preferred timing (post-appointment, post-treatment completion, 48-hour follow-up)

**Output:**
- 10 HIPAA-safe review request messages (email + SMS variants; zero PHI, zero implied medical outcome)
- 10 review response templates: 5 positive (not just "thanks!"), 5 negative (de-escalation + private follow-up offer)
- 3-touch review request sequence for no-response patients
- Review platform strategy (which platform to prioritize, why)
- HIPAA compliance checklist for all review responses (what to NEVER say in a public response)

---

### Prompt 4 — 30-Day Social Media Calendar
**Best for:** Instagram, Facebook, Google Posts — organic patient education and trust building

**Input:**
- Practice specialty and top services
- Target patient demographics
- Any upcoming events, health observances, or promotions
- Brand tone (clinical authority vs. warm community vs. premium boutique)

**Output:**
- 30-day calendar (weekday posts only, 4–5/week)
- Post types: patient education, myth-busting, staff spotlight, behind-the-scenes, testimonial prompt, health awareness, seasonal
- Full copy for 15 posts (others as prompts/themes)
- Hashtag sets by post type (general health + specialty-specific + local)
- 3 Instagram Stories sequences (FAQ, before/after education, team intro)
- HIPAA content rules for healthcare social (what requires patient consent, what doesn't)
