## Unique Differentiators

1. **HIPAA compliance built into every prompt** — flags what can/can't appear in public-facing content
2. **Review response HIPAA checklist** — what NEVER to say in a public response (most practices violate this)
3. **FAQ schema markup** on service pages — immediate SEO value
4. **Review platform strategy** — which platform to prioritize based on specialty (Google vs Healthgrades vs Zocdoc)
5. **No health claims enforcement** — prompts designed to comply with FTC/FDA healthcare advertising standards
6. **Specialty-aware** — different output tone for dental vs. med spa vs. urgent care vs. mental health
