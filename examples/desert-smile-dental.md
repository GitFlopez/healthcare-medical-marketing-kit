### Intake FAQ

**Q: What should I bring to my first appointment?**  
A: Please bring a valid photo ID, your dental insurance card (if applicable), and a list of any current medications. If you have recent dental X-rays from a previous dentist, we'd love a copy — it saves you from a repeat set and helps us understand your history.

**Q: Does Desert Smile Dental accept my insurance?**  
A: We accept most PPO dental plans including Delta Dental, MetLife, Cigna, and many others. We are NOT an HMO provider. Our front desk team will verify your benefits before your visit at no charge — just provide your insurance info when booking.

**Q: What if I don't have dental insurance?**  
A: No problem. We offer an in-house Smile Savings Plan starting at $29/month that covers your cleanings, exams, and X-rays with a discount on all other services. Ask the front desk for details.

**Q: How long will my first appointment take?**  
A: First visits typically run 60–90 minutes. This includes X-rays, a comprehensive exam with Dr. Chen or Dr. Webb, a cleaning (if no additional concerns are found), and time to review your treatment plan if any follow-up is needed.

**Q: Is parking available?**  
A: Yes — free parking in the Green Valley Ranch professional plaza. We're in Building C, Suite 110. Look for the Desert Smile Dental sign near the corner of Green Valley Pkwy and Horizon Ridge.

**Q: What if I'm nervous about dental work?**  
A: You're not alone — dental anxiety is extremely common, and we're experienced in working with nervous patients. Please tell us when you book or when you arrive. We can walk you through every step before we do it, offer nitrous oxide sedation (laughing gas), or simply take things at a slower pace. You're always in control.

**Q: What is your cancellation policy?**  
A: We ask for 48-hour notice for cancellations and reschedules. Life happens — just call or text us and we'll take care of it. Same-day cancellations may incur a $35 missed appointment fee for new patients.

**Q: How do I access the patient portal?**  
A: You'll receive an email from Dental Intel after booking with a link to create your patient portal account. You can complete paperwork, view treatment plans, and message our team through the portal.

**Q: What COVID/hygiene protocols are in place?**  
A: Our office exceeds ADA and CDC infection control standards. We use hospital-grade sterilization for all instruments, HEPA air filtration in every treatment room, and follow all current OSHA guidelines.

**Q: Do you see children?**  
A: Yes! We see patients of all ages. For children under 5, we recommend a first visit by their first birthday or when their first tooth appears. We have a kid-friendly waiting area and our team is trained in pediatric behavior management.

### HIPAA Review Compliance Checklist (Top 5 of 10)

1. **NEVER confirm the reviewer is a patient.** Not even indirectly. Say "Thank you for your feedback" not "Thank you for choosing us for your dental care."
2. **NEVER reference the date, time, or nature of a visit.** Even "your appointment last Tuesday" is a HIPAA violation if it identifies a healthcare interaction.
3. **NEVER respond to a patient who discloses their own condition.** Even if they wrote "I came in for my root canal" — your response cannot acknowledge it.
4. **NEVER say "I'm sorry you felt that way."** This implies the patient's complaint has merit; use "I'm sorry your experience didn't meet your expectations" instead.
5. **NEVER respond with detailed treatment information** even if the patient mentions it first. Their public post doesn't constitute HIPAA authorization for your response.
