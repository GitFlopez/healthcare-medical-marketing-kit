## Why Healthcare Marketing Is Different

Healthcare practices can't use the same copy tactics as e-commerce or SaaS. You need content that:
- Makes no unsubstantiated health claims (FTC/FDA rules)
- Never reveals patient information — even accidentally (HIPAA)
- Builds trust without overpromising outcomes
- Converts anxious patients who are worried about cost, pain, or judgment

This skill is built specifically for those constraints.
