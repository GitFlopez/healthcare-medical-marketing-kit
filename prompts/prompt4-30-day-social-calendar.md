# Prompt 4 — 30-Day Social Media Calendar

## Purpose
Generate a complete 30-day social media content calendar for a healthcare practice — with post copy, hashtags, and content guidance that builds patient trust, educates the community, and grows local visibility without ever risking HIPAA violations.

## HIPAA Social Media Rules
Healthcare practices cannot post photos, videos, or content that could identify a patient without written HIPAA authorization (not just verbal consent). This applies even to "before/after" educational posts if a patient is identifiable. This prompt produces only non-PHI content: staff spotlights, health education, myth-busting, and practice culture posts.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a healthcare social media strategist. Generate a complete 30-day content calendar for a medical practice.

PRACTICE DETAILS:
- Practice name: [PRACTICE NAME]
- Specialty: [SPECIALTY]
- Location: [City, Neighborhood]
- Target patient demographic: [e.g., "families with children", "adults 35–65", "women interested in aesthetics"]
- Top 3 services to highlight: [LIST 3 SERVICES]
- Any upcoming events or health awareness months: [e.g., National Dental Hygiene Month, Breast Cancer Awareness, Back-to-School]
- Brand tone: [warm community / clinical authority / premium boutique]
- Platforms: [Instagram + Facebook / Instagram only / Facebook + Google Posts]

Generate the following:

1. 30-DAY CONTENT CALENDAR (weekdays only, 4–5 posts/week)
   Format for each entry:
   - Day # | Date | Post Type | Platform | Theme | Status (draft/prompt)
   
   Post type rotation (use each regularly):
   - Patient Education: "Did you know...?" facts, myth-busting, prevention tips
   - Service Spotlight: One service, one patient benefit, soft CTA
   - Staff Spotlight: Team member feature (no patient info — staff content only)
   - Behind the Scenes: Day in the life, equipment, office culture
   - Health Awareness: National health observances relevant to the specialty
   - Community: Local event tie-in, neighborhood reference, Las Vegas/city connection
   - Testimonial Prompt: Encourage followers to share their experience (no PHI in prompt)
   - FAQ Answer: Answer one common patient question in post format
   - Seasonal: Back-to-school, benefits expiring, summer health tips, etc.

2. FULL COPY FOR 15 POSTS (others listed as theme + bullet points)
   Each full post:
   - Platform-specific format (Instagram: visual description + caption | Facebook: longer narrative | Google Posts: 100–300 chars)
   - Caption with hook, body, and CTA
   - Hashtag set (5–10 tags: general health + specialty-specific + local)
   - Content note (what visual/image to pair with this post)

3. INSTAGRAM STORIES SEQUENCES (3 sequences, 3–5 slides each)
   Sequence 1: "Meet the Team" — staff intro series
   Sequence 2: "Common Questions About [Top Service]" — FAQ in slides
   Sequence 3: "Your First Visit" — anxiety-reducing walkthrough (no patient images)
   Each slide: text content + suggested background/design note

4. HASHTAG STRATEGY
   - Primary hashtag set (5 tags to use on every post)
   - Specialty hashtags (10 tags specific to the practice type)
   - Local hashtags (5–8 Las Vegas/city-specific tags)
   - Campaign hashtags (3 branded hashtag options for the practice)

5. HIPAA SOCIAL CONTENT RULES (10 rules)
   What requires written patient authorization before posting
   What is safe to post without authorization
   Red flags to review before hitting "post"
   Policy for responding to patient comments on social media
```
